Meishin Muso Ryu

Meishin Muso Ryu (明神無想流) is an Iaido style passed down from Shinkage Ryu (神影流). Our head is Matsushita Shuji Sensei.

We teach Iaido and Kendo classes. Our dojo is in the Poconos region of Pennsylvania. We invite you to train with us.
